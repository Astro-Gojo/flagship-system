**Session Type:** Micro Session



**Topic:** Split Brain Prevention



**Concepts Learned:**

* Split brain occurs when multiple groups believe they are authoritative.
* Conflicting leaders can corrupt data and decisions.
* Quorum ensures only the majority side continues operating.



**Key Insights:**

* Multiple authorities create inconsistency.
* Coordination requires a single authoritative side.
* Majority-based control prevents split brain.



**Systems Engineering Principle:**

Reliable distributed systems maintain consistency by ensuring that authority belongs to only one majority group.
