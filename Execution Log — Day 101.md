**Session Type:** Micro Session



**Topic:** Lost Updates



**Concept:**

Operations like read-modify-write are not atomic and can lead to race conditions when executed by multiple threads.



**Key Insight:**

Multiple-step operations can interleave and cause incorrect results.





**Systems Engineering Principle:**

Robust systems require atomicity, proper synchronization, and efficient design.
