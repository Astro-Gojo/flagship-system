**Session Type:** Core Session



**Topic:** Busy Waiting vs Blocking



**Concept:**

Busy waiting continuously checks a condition while consuming CPU, whereas blocking pauses the thread until the required event or resource becomes available.



**Key Learnings:**

* Busy waiting wastes CPU cycles during waiting
* Blocking improves CPU utilization and scalability
* Busy waiting may still help for extremely short waits
* Waiting strategies involve performance trade-offs



**Core Insight:**

Efficient systems minimize wasted CPU time during synchronization and waiting.



**Outcome:**

* Strengthened understanding of synchronization efficiency
* Connected waiting behavior with system performance
* Improved reasoning about resource-efficient concurrency design
