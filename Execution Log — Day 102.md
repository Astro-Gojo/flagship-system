**Topic:** Atomic vs Non-Atomic Operations



**Concept:**

High-level code may appear atomic but is executed as multiple low-level steps.



**Key Insight:**

Locks and CAS are required to ensure atomicity and correctness.





**Systems Engineering Principle:**

Robust systems require atomicity, proper synchronization, and efficient design.
