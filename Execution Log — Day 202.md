**Concepts Learned;**

**Key Insights:**

* Eager loading retrieves related data immediately.
* It reduces future requests but increases startup time.
* Engineers choose between eager and lazy loading based on application needs.



**Systems Engineering Principle:**

Sometimes performing work earlier simplifies future operations.



**Progression:**

Lazy Loading → Eager Loading
