**Session Type: Core Session**



**Topic:** Fairness Mechanisms (Starvation Prevention)



**Concept:**

Fairness mechanisms ensure that all threads get a chance to execute, preventing starvation.



**Key Mechanisms:**

* FIFO (First-In-First-Out) scheduling
* Fair semaphores (ordered wake-up)
* Aging (priority increases over time)



**Key Learnings:**

* Normal synchronization ensures correctness but not fairness
* Starvation occurs due to lack of scheduling fairness
* Fairness must be explicitly designed into systems



**Trade-off:**

* Fairness can reduce performance due to strict ordering



**Core Insight:**

System design requires balancing correctness, safety, coordination, and fairness.



**Outcome:**

* Understood how real systems prevent starvation
* Learned trade-offs between fairness and performance
* Strengthened multi-layered system thinking
