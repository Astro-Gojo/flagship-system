**Session Type:** Micro Session



**Topic:** Graceful Degradation



**Concepts Learned:**

* Non-essential features can be sacrificed.
* Core functionality should remain available.
* Small failures should not become complete outages.



**Key Insights:**

* Availability is often more important than perfection.
* Systems should degrade gracefully rather than catastrophically.
* Survivability is a design goal.



**Systems Engineering Principle:**

Reliable systems prioritize continuity over completeness.



**Progression:**

Systems understanding expanded from:

* failover

→ recovery

→ graceful degradation

→ survivability
