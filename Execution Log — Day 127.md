**Session Type:** Core Session



**Topic:** Multi-Level Feedback Queue (MLFQ)



**Concept:**

MLFQ is a dynamic scheduling algorithm where tasks move between queues based on their behavior.



**Key Learnings:**

* Tasks are not fixed to one queue
* CPU-bound tasks move to lower priority
* I/O-bound tasks stay at higher priority
* Priority boosting prevents starvation



**Core Insight:**

Adaptive scheduling improves both performance and fairness.



**Outcome:**

* Understood dynamic scheduling behavior
* Learned how systems adapt to workload patterns
* Strengthened advanced scheduling knowledge
