**Concepts Learned:**

* Failover transfers responsibility from failed components to healthy backups.
* Heartbeats detect failures.
* Leader election selects replacements.

**Key Insights:**

* Single points of failure are dangerous bottlenecks.
* Automatic recovery reduces downtime.
* Backup components enable continuous operation.



**Systems Engineering Principle:**
