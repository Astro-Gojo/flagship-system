**Topic:** Checkpointing



**Concepts Learned:**

* Checkpoints save progress periodically.
* Recovery resumes from recent state instead of starting over.
* Checkpointing reduces wasted work.



**Key Insights:**

* Recomputing everything after failures is inefficient.
* Recovery speed is an important part of reliability.
* Fault tolerance improves when progress is preserved.



**Systems Engineering Principle:**

Reliable systems preserve progress so failures do not force unnecessary repetition.
