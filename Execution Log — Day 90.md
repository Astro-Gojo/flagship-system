**Session Type: Micro Session**



**Topic: System Capacity and Load Management**



**Concept:**

Execution must adapt to system capacity. Overloading leads to poor performance and instability.



**Insight:**

Just like computer systems, humans have limits in energy, focus, and performance.



**Key Principle:**

If the system is overloaded, reduce load or delay tasks instead of forcing execution.



**Application:**

Avoid pushing heavy work during fatigue. Maintain consistency through smaller actions.



**Systems Engineering Principle:**

Effective systems balance performance and stability by respecting capacity limits.
