**Session Type:** Micro Session



**Topic:** Heartbeats \& Failure Detection



**Concepts Learned:**

* Heartbeats are periodic signals exchanged between machines.
* Missing heartbeats indicate possible failures.
* Heartbeats support cluster management and leader monitoring.



**Key Insights:**

* Machines cannot be assumed to remain alive indefinitely.
* Failure detection is fundamental to reliability.
* Large distributed systems require continuous health monitoring.



**Systems Engineering Principle:**

Reliable systems continuously verify assumptions instead of trusting that components remain healthy forever.



**Progression:**

Systems understanding expanded from:

* timeouts

→ failure detection

→ heartbeats

→ cluster monitoring
