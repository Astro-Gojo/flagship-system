**Session Type:** Core Session



**Topic:** Amdahl’s Law



**Concept:**

Amdahl’s Law states that system speedup is limited by the portion of the system that cannot be improved.



**Key Learnings:**

* Optimization has diminishing returns
* Non-improved parts limit maximum speedup
* Even infinite improvement in one part cannot overcome system constraints



**Core Insight:**

Maximum system performance is constrained by its slowest, non-optimized components.



**Outcome:**

* Understood limits of optimization
* Connected bottlenecks with performance limits
* Strengthened high-level system thinking
