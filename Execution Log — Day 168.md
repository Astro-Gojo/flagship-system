**Day 168 — Execution Log | Date : 07/06/2026**



**Session Type:** Micro Session



**Topic:** Database Indexing



**Concepts Learned:**

* Indexes accelerate searches and lookups.
* Indexes avoid full scans of data.
* Faster reads are achieved at the cost of additional storage and slower writes.



**Key Insights:**

* Reads become faster because the system maintains extra structures.
* Updating these structures during writes introduces overhead.



**Systems Engineering Principle:**

Performance improvements often involve trade-offs rather than free gains.



**Progression:**

Distributed systems understanding expanded from:

* load balancing

→ indexing

→ efficient data access
