**Key Insights:**

* One machine eventually reaches hardware limits.
* Sharding distributes both storage and workload.
* Every scaling solution introduces new engineering trade-offs.
* Intelligent data distribution is essential to prevent new bottlenecks.



**Systems Engineering Principle:**

Every optimization shifts the bottleneck rather than eliminating it. Good engineering is the art of choosing where those bottlenecks should exist.



**Progression:**
