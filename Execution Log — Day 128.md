**Session Type:** Core Session



**Topic:** Load vs Utilization



**Concept:**

Load represents the amount of work entering a system, while utilization represents how much of the system’s capacity is being used.



**Key Learnings:**

* Load and utilization are different but related
* High load does not always mean high utilization
* Systems must balance demand and capacity
* Both underutilization and overutilization are inefficient



**Core Insight:**

Efficient systems operate at an optimal balance between load and utilization.



**Outcome:**

* Understood system capacity vs demand
* Strengthened system-level performance thinking
* Improved ability to evaluate system efficiency
