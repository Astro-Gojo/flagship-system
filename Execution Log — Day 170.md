**Day 170 — Execution Log | Date : 09/06/2026**



**Session Type:** Micro Session



**Topic:** Backpressure



**Concepts Learned:**

* Producers may generate work faster than consumers can process it.
* Unlimited queues lead to overload.
* Backpressure slows producers to maintain system stability.



**Key Insights:**

* Preventing overload is often more important than maximizing production speed.
* Systems must regulate themselves to avoid collapse.



**Systems Engineering Principle:**

Sustainable systems control load instead of allowing unlimited growth.



**Progression:**

Scalability understanding expanded from:

* throughput

→ queue growth

→ overload prevention

→ backpressure mechanisms
