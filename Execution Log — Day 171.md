**Day 171 — Execution Log | Date : 10/06/2026**



**Session Type:** Micro Session



**Topic:** Idempotency



**Concepts Learned:**

* Idempotent operations produce the same result when repeated.
* Distributed systems frequently retry requests.
* Safe retries are essential for reliability.



**Key Insights:**

* Networks are unreliable.
* Duplicate requests are normal rather than exceptional.
* Idempotency prevents repeated execution from causing incorrect results.



**Systems Engineering Principle:**

Reliable systems assume failures and retries are inevitable and are designed to tolerate them safely.



**Progression:**

Distributed systems understanding expanded from:

* backpressure

→ retries

→ duplicate requests

→ idempotent operations
