**Day 129 — Execution Log** **| Date : 29/04/2026**



**Session Type:** Core Session



**Topic:** Bottlenecks



**Concept:**

A bottleneck is the component that limits the overall performance of a system.



**Key Learnings:**

* System performance is limited by the slowest component
* Optimizing non-bottleneck parts has little impact
* Bottlenecks can exist in CPU, memory, disk, network, or synchronization



**Core Insight:**

Effective optimization requires identifying and improving the bottleneck.



**Correction Made:**

* Learned that improving non-bottleneck components results in minimal performance gain



**Outcome:**

* Developed ability to identify system limitations
* Strengthened performance diagnosis skills
* Improved optimization decision-making
